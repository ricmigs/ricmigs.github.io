About

Hello there! Welcome to my personal page. My name is Ricardo, and I'm a 28-year-old hailing from the vibrant city of Lisbon, Portugal. Life has granted me the opportunity to explore my passions and interests, and I'm excited to share them with you.




I have a passion for various forms of art and entertainment, including photography, reading, and immersing myself in the world of music. I believe that laughter is one of the best things in life, and I thoroughly enjoy watching different TV shows, especially comedy series.

I draw inspiration from the people around me and constantly strive to better myself in all aspects of life. Curiosity is one of my defining traits, and I find joy in exploring a wide range of subjects, embracing general knowledge and cultural diversity.

My academic journey led me to pursue a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Linguistics, with my Master's degree being completed in 2022, where I've studied the impact of prosodic and gestural development in later stages of development. During my time as a student, I had the opportunity to work as a researcher in the fascinating field of language acquisition, delving into the intricacies of how infants learn and process language.

Recently, I embarked on a new professional adventure and joined a dynamic company as a data quality intern. This role allows me to leverage my analytical skills and contribute to ensuring accurate and reliable data within the organization.

On this personal page, I aim to share my experiences, insights, and reflections on various topics that pique my interest.
